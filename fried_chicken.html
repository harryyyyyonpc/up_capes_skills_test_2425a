How To Make Fried Chicken?

In this day and age, nothing ever beats a crispy fried chicken.

Do you want to taste one too? Let's get ready to cook!

Ingredients and Tools:

  • drumsticks or chicken wings
  • cooking oil
  • flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • bowl
  • plate
  • eggs
  • nonstick pan

Steps:

  1. Whisk the eggs in the bowl.
  2. Dredge the chicken in the flour mixture.
  3. Put the chicken in the egg mixture.
  4. Put the chicken on the breadcrumb pile.
  5. Heat your nonstick pan.
  6. Pour cooking oil on the pan.
  7. Put the chicken on the pan.
  8. Wait for the chicken to be golden brown.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy eating!
