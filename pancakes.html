How To Make Pancakes?

Pancakes are one of the delicious foods that we eat in our breakfasts.

Having some trouble in creating a perfect pancake? Prepare your ingredients and let's get started!

Ingredients and Tools:

  • 1 box of pancake mix
  • measuring cup
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • butter
  • bowl
  • nonstick pan

Steps:

  1. Pour the pancake mix into the bowl.
  2. Slowly add the water into the pancake mix.
  3. Heat your nonstick pan.
  4. Melt the butter on the pan.
  5. Pour the pancake batter onto the pan using a measuring cup.
  6. Wait until the bubbles begin to form on the edges and in the center of the pancake.
  7. If bubbles formed, flip the pancake.
  8. Wait for the pancakes to be golden brown.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy!
