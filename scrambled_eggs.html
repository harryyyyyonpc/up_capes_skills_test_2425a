How To Make Scrambled Eggs?

Any breakfast will not be complete without having eggs in our dish.

Do you want to know how to create the most delicious scrambled eggs ever? Let's get ready to cook!

Ingredients and Tools:

  • Eggs
  • Bowl
  • Whisk
  • Butter or Oil
  • Frying Pan

Steps:

  1. Put the eggs on the bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs.
  3. Melt the butter on the frying pan.
  4. Put the eggs on the frying pan.
  5. Wait until the eggs turn golden brown.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
