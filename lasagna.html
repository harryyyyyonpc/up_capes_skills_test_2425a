How To Make Lasagna?

I can feel that you crave for something Italian, something delicious.

If you are reading this, this is the sign for you to make a lasagna!

Ingredients and Tools:

  • ground beef
  • bell peppers
  • onions
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • crushed tomatoes
  • ricotta
  • shredded mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • noodles
  • oven

Steps:

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan ready.
  3. Put a layer of noodles on a container, then a layer of meat sauce, and repeat it three times.
  4. Bake it in the oven until bubbly
  5. You are now ready to eat!
